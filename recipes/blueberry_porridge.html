Blueberry porridge

Description

This microwaved version is easy and fast to make. Also very nutritiously packed with proteins. Ideal for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Rolled oats
  • Blueberries (or other preferred berries)

Steps

  1. Mix the milk with the rolled oats into a microwave safe bowl.
  2. Stir and then cook on high in the microwave for 2 and a half minutes.
  3. Remove from the microwave and stir.
  4. Place back into microwave and cook on high for thirty seconds.
  5. Repeat the previous two steps until the porridge has your desired consistency.
  6. Add blueberries and stir.
