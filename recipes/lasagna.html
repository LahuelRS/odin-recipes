Lasagna

Description

This plate is an all time loved one by everyone.
It consists in layers of pasta (lasagna noodles) and other kinds of stuffings such as meats or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Meat or Soy-based meat.
  • Onion and garlic.
  • Tomato sauce.
  • Sugar and salt.
  • Lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses.
  • Eggs.

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions (check the packaging)
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
