Pizza - two ingredients dough version

Description

Who doesn't love pizza?. Having an italian origin and being a worldwide favourite, it consists in bread with a topping (usually tomato sauce and cheese over it).

Ingredients

  • Self-rising flour.
  • Yogurt (plain greek)

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients.
  2. Knead for eight to ten minutes.
  3. Parbake the crust for five to seven minutes.
  4. Add sauce and cheese on top of crust
  5. Return pizza to oven and bake until the crust is slightly browned.
